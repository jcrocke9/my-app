Compiled in this Google Spreadsheet is information for out-of-district volunteers about the congressional races closest to DC where Democrats are fighting to take back the House. This is an *active* document so check back for updates! All of the Democratic campaigns on this list need your help, especially the "high" priority races which are very competitive. Consider donating, volunteering, and working to flip congress this fall.

25 Campaigns

Scan the list of "Towns in District" and see if you know people who live there. Should you have a connection to any of these districts (like local friends or family), *reach out* and make sure those connections are ready to vote in November. Maybe you could help your local connections host an event or otherwise support and encourage their actions. And go spend a few weekends with them, knocking on doors or volunteering at a local campaign office - it makes a difference!

Regardless if you have a prior connection to a district, you can still make a personal connection to a Democratic candidate and a campaign. All of these campaigns would *love* for you to help them. Watch their biographical videos, read about them - they are amazing! And they need money, they need canvassers, they need cheerleaders. Adopt one and help us all in the effort to flip the House!

Apart from the campaigns themselves, there are many ways to connect to more organized groups:

  • Swing Left, focused on swing districts with detailed guides on how to pitch in. Put in an address to find (or host) ongoing events, join local google groups, etc. Sign up for their pledges, ongoing initiatives and grassroots activities! https://swingleft.org/
  • Indivisible - national movement to resist Trump, please join a local chapter to stay engaged and informed on legislative priorities! While many hey have also set up a great system for phone bank opportunities to all competitive senate races. https://www.indivisible.org/
  • Virginia Grassroots - coalition of local groups by district in Virginia. Very effective for get out the vote efforts in 2017 - be sure to check out the individual groups http://virginiagrassroots.org/vgr-organizations.php and http://virginiagrassroots.org/index.php
  • Blue Wave NJ - sign up for specific canvassing dates through their website
  • Turn PA Blue - sign up for phone banking, canvassing, *hosting* canvassers etc. https://turnpablue.org/take-action/phone-banking/
  • Mobilize America, a platform used by many campaigns to organize events. Search by day or within a distance of a zip code https://events.mobilizeamerica.io/
  • Red2Blue, a national organization mobilizes volunteers and connects them to campaigns to elect Democratic candidates at all levels. Especially good for those who don’t want to canvass. https://red2blue.org/
  • Sister District Project organizes volunteers into local teams to win state elections for Democrats. They "sister" this deep blue capacity with swing districts to support strategic state races that matter. https://www.sisterdistrict.com/
  • Of course the Democratic Congressional Campaign Committee itself https://dccc.org/
